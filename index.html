About the Project

This Airbus A320 cockpit model is a hands-on representation created by Avionics First Year students. It highlights cockpit layout familiarity, build discipline, and presentation flow—built for exhibits and learning.

Purpose

To demonstrate cockpit organization and guide visitors through key zones: instrument panel, overhead panel, and center pedestal—explained in a clear, tour-friendly way.

Learning through building

Craftsmanship

Built with careful measurement and alignment—aimed to look clean on camera and strong in real exhibit setup.

Presentation-ready finish

Teamwork

Tasks were split by cockpit sections and system features—like real avionics teamwork, but student-powered.
